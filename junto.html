Drowning in digital escapes? So were we.

The world tells you to escape into a screen. We say the real power is right here. Negative peer pressure is real, but what if you could turn it into your greatest strength?

Welcome to Nova. Your tool for the real world.

Nova is a lightweight web app that connects your real-world actions with your team. It's about positive peer pressure, safe support, and learning what's really upin 30 seconds or less.

Instead of fighting peer pressure, we gamify positive peer pressure. Connect real-world actions to team-based rewards and safe peer support.
